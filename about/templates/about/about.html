About Us

Founded in August 2024 by solicitors Hannah Unger, Carrie McMeel and Demi Mullen, Dog Law Ireland (DLÍ) is dedicated to making dog law accessible and understood by all.

Our Purpose

We cannot change what we don’t understand, so we want to make dog law accessible and easily understood by everyone – the general public, animal rescues, An Garda Síochána, the Government, the veterinary community, the legal profession, and all those working in the canine industry.

While we are small in number, we are determined to make a change. We've built wonderful relationships with advocates who have long fought for Ireland’s dogs, and we're grateful to the volunteers who support us. If you’d like to get involved, especially as a legal researcher — please reach out!
